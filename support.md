### **Create Your First Habit**

1.  Open Steady.
2.  Go to Habits **Add Habit (+)**.
3.  Enter the name of your habit.
4.  Assign it to specific days.
5.  Save and start tracking.

### **Mark Habit as Completed**

-   Go to Daily
-   Tap the square next to a habit.
-   A filled square means the habit is completed for the day.

### **Edit or Delete a Habit**

1.  Tap a habit to open its detail view.
2.  Edit name, schedule, or icon.
3.  To delete: tap **Delete Habit** in the detail screen.

## ğŸ›  Troubleshooting

### **The app doesn't save changes**

-   Ensure your device has available storage.
-   Restart the app.

### **Habits are not appearing today**

-   Check the habit's schedule.
-   Confirm today is a selected day for that habit.

### **Streak reset unexpectedly**

-   Streak resets if a scheduled day passes without marking the habit
    completed.
-   Check if the habit schedule is correct.
