# Privacy Policy
_Last updated: November 16, 2025_

This Privacy Policy describes how the Steady app (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information.  
All user data — including habits, activities, schedules, scores, and app settings — is stored **locally on your device** only.

We do not:

- Create user accounts  
- Store data on external servers  
- Track users  
- Use analytics or crash logging tools  
- Access contacts, photos, camera, microphone, location, or other sensors  

## 2. Purchases and Subscriptions

Steady uses Apple’s **StoreKit** for in-app purchases.  
All billing information is handled solely by **Apple**.  

We do **not** have access to:

- Your full payment details  
- Your Apple ID email  
- Your purchase history beyond what Apple provides to confirm whether you have an active subscription

Apple’s own privacy policy applies to all transactions made through the App Store.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing

Because we do not collect personal data, we do **not** sell, rent, or share any personal information with third parties.

## 4. Children’s Privacy

Steady does not collect any personal data from children or adults.  
The app is safe for all ages because no identifiable information is gathered.

## 5. Data Security

All app data remains on your device.  
If you delete the the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered by us.

## 6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information for us to provide, modify, or delete.  
You maintain full control over your data on your own device.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
